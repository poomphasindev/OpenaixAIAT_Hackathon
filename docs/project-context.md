# Project Context

## Hackathon

OpenAI Codex x AIAT Hackathon Thailand, May 6-9, 2026.

Theme: Wellness AI in the Post-AGI Era.

The idea should fit "Wellness for All" and matter in a post-AGI world: personalized care, mental wellbeing, personal growth, elderly care, public health, ethics, and human-AI collaboration.

## Product

RoutineSense AI is a daily wellness and energy copilot.

It helps a person check in by text or voice, turns messy daily stories into structured signals, compares those signals against a seven-day starter memory, and returns one timely micro-plan with an inspectable reason.

## Winning Positioning

Do not pitch as a burnout detector, diagnosis tool, or therapy replacement.

Pitch as:

> RoutineSense learns what silently breaks your energy, then helps you act before the day goes off track.

## MVP Loop

Morning:
- 15-30 second text or voice check-in.
- The app returns one intention, one boundary, and one fallback plan.

Afternoon:
- Optional adaptive nudge when today resembles a known rough pattern.

Evening:
- Reflection.
- Structured tags are extracted.
- User marks whether the recommendation helped.

Weekly:
- Seven-day starter memory summary with uncertainty and user confirmation.

## Must-Have Demo Features

- Smart check-in.
- One-card daily plan.
- "Why this suggestion?" evidence card.
- Seven-day starter memory.
- Helpful / not helpful feedback.
- Privacy and safety controls.

## Five Golden Demo Scenarios

1. Low sleep + high AQI + low energy.
2. Caffeine loop day.
3. Overloaded workday.
4. Good balanced day.
5. Crisis-language safety routing.

## Judge Answer

When asked "what is the AI effect?", answer:

The AI effect is structured understanding of messy voice or text, personal memory across days, and timely micro-interventions tailored to the individual rather than generic wellness advice.
